Open Positions: Are you ready to start your PhD in August this year?

Are you passionate about creating and optimizing computational codes specifically for atmospheric science and artificial intelligence applications?

Are you interested in combining artificial intelligence techniques and atmospheric science?

Are you looking forward to working in the newly established Atmospheric Modeling Center in Lahti, Finland?

If you answered all the above questions with a `yes,' please send your

• Motivation letter,

• CV (one page),

• List of publications (if available), and

• Contacts of two references

to Michael Boy (michael.boy@helsinki.fi) and Zhi-Song Liu (zhisong.liu@lut.fi) by the 30th of April 2024, at the latest. We invite possible candidates to a Zoom meeting at the beginning of May.
